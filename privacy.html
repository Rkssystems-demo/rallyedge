Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 17, 2026

The short version RallyEdge collects only the data needed to make the app work for you. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. Your tennis journal is yours.

1. Who We Are

RallyEdge is a sports performance app for tennis players, built and operated by Rachel Seifer. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at [email protected].

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use RallyEdge:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth)
  • Tennis profile — skill level, dominant hand, play style, league name, home club, and other optional profile fields you choose to fill in
  • Journal entries — match results, lesson notes, practice sessions, scores, ratings, and notes you log in the app
  • Opponent records — names, skill levels, strengths, weaknesses, and notes you add about opponents in the Scout tab
  • Goals — improvement goals and progress you track in the Goals tab
  • Gear — equipment information you log in the Gear tab

We do not collect payment information at this time. All data you enter is associated with your account and stored securely.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the RallyEdge app experience:

  • To display your journal, stats, goals, and gear back to you within the app
  • To generate AI-powered features such as pep talks, opponent game plans, and goal tips (when enabled)
  • To calculate your season stats and performance summaries
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns (no personally identifiable information is used for this purpose)

4. AI Features

RallyEdge uses the Anthropic Claude API to power AI features including personalized pep talks, opponent game plans, and goal coaching tips. When you use these features, relevant portions of your tennis data (such as recent match history, opponent notes, and active goals) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is processed solely to generate your response and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

AI features are optional. If you choose not to use them, no data is sent to Anthropic.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. All data is protected by Row Level Security — meaning each user can only access their own data. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party marketing services. We use the following service providers to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI features (only when you use AI features)
  • Expo / Apple — app distribution via TestFlight and the App Store

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of RallyEdge, its users, or others.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected] and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

RallyEdge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

We're happy to answer any questions about your privacy or how RallyEdge handles your data.
